## Project Title

**Nexus: Echoes of Empire** — A turn-based tactical strategy game set in a fractured post-colonial world.

## Pitch

*Nexus: Echoes of Empire* is a mid-scale, single-player campaign strategy game where players lead one of four factions competing for control of a shattered continent. Combat combines traditional tactical grid-based movement with a dynamic resource economy that rewards long-term planning over moment-to-moment optimization.

## Core Pillars

| Pillar | Description | Why It Matters |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Tactical Depth** | Turn-based grid combat with unit positioning, elevation, and ability synergies | Casual to hardcore players both enjoy mastery through practice |
| **Emergent Strategy** | Resource scarcity forces hard choices; no "optimal" build path | Replayability; players discover different approaches each run |
| **Narrative Integration** | Campaign missions tied to faction lore and character arcs | Story justifies gameplay challenges and world-building |
| **Aesthetic Consistency** | Hand-crafted isometric art paired with orchestral soundtrack | Memorable, cohesive experience; distances from procedural/AI-generated feel |

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Strategy game enthusiasts (Civilization, XCOM, Fire Emblem)
- **Age:** 16+
- **Platforms:** PC (Windows, Linux, macOS)
- **Playtime:** 30–50 hours per campaign

## Key Features

1. **Four Playable Factions** — each with unique units, abilities, and campaign narratives
2. **Campaign Mode** — 15–20 mission story arc with branching choices
3. **Tactical Combat** — grid-based movement, line-of-sight, destructible environments
4. **Tech Tree & Progression** — unlock new units and abilities through resource investment
5. **Dynamic Economy** — production queues, supply lines, and trade networks
6. **Mod Support** — map editor, unit/ability creators for community content

## Success Criteria

- **Critical:** Campaign playable soup-to-nuts without game-breaking bugs
- **Gameplay:** At least 3 distinct winning strategies per mission
- **Content:** 15+ unique unit types across all factions
- **Polish:** All audio custom-composed; all visuals hand-created (no AI)
- **Replayability:** Campaign completion achievable in 30–50 hours; 4 faction routes
